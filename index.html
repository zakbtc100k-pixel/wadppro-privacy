1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how WA Dp Pro ("the App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This policy complies with Google Play's User Data policy and applicable data protection regulations.

2. Information We Collect

WA Dp Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect MINIMAL data.

A) Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • We do NOT access, upload, store, or transmit your photos or images
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT use advertising identifiers (Ad ID)
  • We do NOT collect contacts, call logs, or messages
  • We do NOT use cookies or web tracking technologies

B) Information We MAY Collect (anonymously):

  • Device Information: Basic device model and OS version for app compatibility
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to improve stability (no personal information)
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous, aggregated statistics (e.g., feature popularity)

3. How Your Photos Are Handled

YOUR PHOTOS NEVER LEAVE YOUR DEVICE.
  • All photo editing and processing occurs 100% locally on your device
  • Photos are loaded into device memory only during editing sessions
  • Saved photos are stored only in your device's gallery/storage
  • Shared photos go directly from your device to the receiving app — never through our servers
  • Temporary cache files are automatically cleaned up
  • We have NO servers that store user images

4. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous information we may collect is used exclusively to:

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand popular features to guide development
  • Ensure compatibility across devices and OS versions

We NEVER use data for advertising profiling, selling to third parties, or identifying individual users.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third party. Anonymous crash data may be processed through standard Android platform services (Google Play Services), which have their own privacy policies.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All user preferences are stored locally using encrypted storage
  • No user data is stored on external servers
  • Android Auto Backup only includes app preferences, never photos

7. Permissions Explained

Camera (Optional): Used ONLY when you tap "Take Selfie." The App works fully without this permission.

Storage / Media Images (Required): Used to access your gallery for photo selection and to save edited images. We only access the specific photo you select.

Internet (Optional use): Used only for anonymous crash reporting. All editing features work completely offline.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App does not target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App contains no age-restricted content, in-app purchases, or social features. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: Request information about any data we hold
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all local data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics via device settings
  • Control: Manage App permissions via device Settings
  • Portability: Edited photos are standard JPEG files in your gallery

10. Data Retention

  • App preferences: Until uninstall or data clear
  • Cache: Auto-cleaned by system
  • Crash reports: Maximum 90 days, then deleted
  • User photos: NEVER retained by us
